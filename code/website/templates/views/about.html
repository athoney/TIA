Meet TIA!

TIA stands for Threat Information Assistant. She is a web application that will help you identify, understand, and remidy your most critical security vulnerabilities.

TIA is the product of a research project housed in the Cybersecurity Education and Research (CEDAR) lab at the University of Wyoming (UW). Development on this project began in Summer 2022 and was developed by a team of UW undergraduate students. Check us out below! TIA blends cybersecurity and software development to create a unique research endeavor with immense potential. Future work will utilize the power of natural language processing to further enhance STIX graphs.
